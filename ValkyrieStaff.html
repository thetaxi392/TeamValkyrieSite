Team Valkyrie

2022 Coaching Staff

Coach Josh Bauer

Coach Bauer founded Team Valkyrie in 2015 after a successful high school coaching career at Mehlville High School. He has also coached for the Missouri Women's National Team since 2015, and has coached the Missouri National Dual Team since 2016.

Coach Samantha Rebentisch

Coach Sam has been a part of Valkyrie since 2019. She wrestled for University of Lindenwood-Belleville in college, and Jonathan Law High School in Milford, Connecticut, where she earned 6th place in State in the boys division at 106 lbs. She also placed 6th at Women's Nationals at 105 lbs. She currently coaches at Fontbonne University.

Coach Emma Valleroy

Coach Emma was a four-year starter at Mehlville High School, and a Missouri National Team Member who then became a Missouri National Team Coach. She was a wrestler for University of Lindenwood-Belleville, and is currently the Head Wrestling Coach at her alma mater. She has coached girls to state medals and the state finals.

Coach Carly Valleroy

Coach Carly was a four year starter at Mehlville High School, and is currently a senior at McKendree University, where she is a returning All-American at 109 lbs. She is a 3x Fargo All-American and has several other AA finishes at USA Folkstyle Nationals and World Team Trials.
