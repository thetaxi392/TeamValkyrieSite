19 December 2021 Entry: Website Up and Running!

Hi! My name is Josh Bauer and I'm the Head Coach for Team Valkyrie. We are an all-girls freestyle wrestling club located in St. Louis, MO. I'm happy that the internet has brought you to this site to find out more information about us, who we are and what we have to offer.

Girls wrestling is the fasting high school and college sport in the United States, and we are here to help young women grow a love for the sport and help them reach their goals, whether it be to win a match, win a title, earn a college scholarship, or gain valuable leadership skills to serve them throughout life. We help girls of all ages and skill levels reach another level within their wrestling.

The biggest benefit to wrestling for Team Valkyrie, however, is the camaraderie. We have had over 50 girls each season since 2019, and we're only getting bigger! Our growth has continued with ZERO ADVERTISING. Most everyone learns about Valkyrie through word-of-mouth, and this is by design. This shows that the community trusts us to help their daughters reach new heights through wrestling. One of the most rewarding things for me as a coach is to see the girls from Valkyrie talk to one another and have fun cheering each other on even though they're from different schools. The bonds the girls share through this club show in their wrestling, because when others have the same goals and support each other, we all succeed and stick through it all!

If you are interested in learning more about Team Valkyrie, please go to out Contact page and join our mailing list, or if you have questions, just fill out the comment section and I will respond to you as soon as possible! Added something
